UniKampus Program

At UniKaksha, we understand that for graduates entering corporate life, it can be a leap into the unknown. We have specially designed our ‘UniKampus Program’ that assists colleges to ensure that their students are Industry-fit and Job-ready the moment they finish graduation. Our goal is to help close skills gaps with industry-driven curriculums designed to help graduates thrive in their workplaces. The teams behind each program are trained professionals who are experts in their fields and work with students daily to ensure that the content of our programs reflects current industry trends by keeping abreast of what’s currently needed from tomorrow’s hires. We have customized our UniKampus Program to serve your needs better: UniKampus ‘IKON’ Program for final semester students UniKampus ‘EMBARK’ Program for first to penultimate semester students
